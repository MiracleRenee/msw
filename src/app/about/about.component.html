THE BEGINNING....

The dictonary defines Miracle as an extraordinary event that can not be explained and considered to be the work of a divine agency. My life story definelty lives up to my name. From birth I was defying odds. My mother gave birth to me at the age of 12 and due to unfortunate events i was removed from her care and placed into foster care. I have overcome sexual, mental and verbal abuse. So as you can imagine I was a child with a lot of "problems".

THE MIDDLE....

I started doing drugs in the sixth grade and school became my last priority. My test scores carried me through school. I became such a menace to society that I was sent to bootcamp. What I thought was the worst thing that could ever happen, turned out to be my saving grace.

The good definelty out weighs the bad. I did graduate HighSchool on time, I've traveled to speak on several pannels advocating for women and foster youth. I have traveled to several fundraisers and different companies to share my testimony. I am also currently working on my first book about my life.

Of course this is my story in a nutshell and if you want to know more I am completely open and transparent and would love to share in hopes that you would be encouraged and lifted above your circumstances to see them at a different perspective so you can aquire the strength to persevere.
